01. Waling Bazar Syangja

Waling, a serene village in Syangja, Nepal, holds a treasure within its midst—Bahunthan. This vantage point offers a mesmerizing view of the burgeoning Waling Smart City, earning it widespread renown among locals and travelers alike. The journey to Bahunthan is an adventure through lush forests and meandering trails, rejuvenating both body and soul. Upon reaching the summit, visitors are greeted by a breathtaking panorama—a sprawling expanse of vibrant cityscape merging with the verdant landscape. As the sun sets, casting a golden glow over the city, Bahunthan becomes a sanctuary, offering solace and tranquility amidst the embrace of nature.
